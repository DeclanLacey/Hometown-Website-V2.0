Welcome To
Des Moines

Enjoy all that the centrally located capital of Iowa has to offer

Visit the greater Des Moines area Botanical Gardens

If you love the outdoors and seeing plant life from all over the world, the Botanical Gardens should certainly be your first stop. With a wide range of plants from every climate imaginable inside the climatron, to beautiful gardens spread out among the grounds that also offer some of the best views of downtown Des Moines you can come by. Check out their website and hours here!

Spend an afternoon at the beautiful Principal Park

Baseball is America's favorite pass time, and the time will fly by watching the Chicago Cubs triple A affiliate play in the beautiful principal park. Whether it's a day game on a warm summer day or a Friday night game watching the fireworks after a Cubs win the ballpark is sure to be fun for the whole family. Try it out for yourself and check out their schedule and buy tickets here!

Indulge in one of Des Moines many wonderful restaurants

After a long day of exploring the city having dinner at one of Des Moines restaurants is the perfect way to spend your evening. The city offers nearly every type of food, whether you are in the mood for Italian, American comfort food, Chinese, Indian, or any other type of food, Des Moines has an option for everyone. Check out a list of recommended restaurants and bars here!

Take a step back into the captivating history of Iowa

Make a trip to the State Historical Building downtown to take deep dive into Iowa's history. Popular among students and adults alike the Historical Center has a plethora of interesting information and captivating diaramas taking you back into the history of the state of Iowa. Check out their hours and other information here!
